about us

Welcome to Thai Restaurant

Welcome to Thai Restaurant, the finest Indian takeaway delivery service based in Stratford E15 2SP. We warmly welcome you to try an elegant Bangladeshi and Indian food experience with superb delivery service. The concept of Thai Restaurant arose from a need to challenge the current norm in local takeaway delivery service with several years of business experience.

Those who are truly interested in the art of “Indian” food will know that majority of the artist are Bangladeshis and the Thai Restaurant of Bengal had greater usage of spices similar to their Indian counterparts.

The Thai Restaurant –a premier home delivery service headed by award winning Chef Judul Syed to bring the best of authentic Thai style of cooking to your door steps. Syed designed the menu to bring out the best of Nawab’s traditions of India and Bengal cooking which lies in harmony with his 20 years of experience.

Chef Judul Syed learned cooking at his very early age from his parents, grandparents and who learned from their ancestor’s served in the court of Thai’s of Bengal; the knowledge was passed through from generation to generation from an unbroken line. He is one of the very few chef in the UK with a speciality of Thai Restaurant court style of cooking.

Chef Syed has worked previously on City Spice of Brighton, Thai Restaurant of Royston, Curry spice of Margate and won many awards for his unique style of cooking; hugely appreciated by their regular patrons.

Thai Restaurant aim to give you food cooked with highest standards, assuring the highest level of hygiene and customer satisfaction and committed to delivery your food in warm and within 20 minutes of cooking into your door steps.

Please Note: We do not use any artificial colouring; we do not use any almonds on any items unless requested by any of our customers.

We offer free delivery within 3 mile radius. Any order for delivery outside of the free delivery zone will cost extra delivery charge.
