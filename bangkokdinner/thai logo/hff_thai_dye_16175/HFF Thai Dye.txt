HFF Thai Dye is based on Siamese, from page 77 of "Special Effects and 
Topical Alphabets: 100 Complete Fonts" by Dan X. Solo, first published by 
Dover Publications in 1978. Siamese is also featured on page 73 0f "The 
Solotype Catalog of 4,147 Display Typefaces".

HFF Thai Dye is a free font. You are free to use this font in any way 
you want to. You can share this font with others if you wished to.

Use freely, share freely and, most of all, enjoy the font.
