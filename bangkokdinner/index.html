Welcome to Bangkok Dinner Restaurant

Welcome to Bangkok Dinner Restaurant, the finest Indian takeaway delivery service based in Stratford E15 2SP. We warmly welcome you to try an elegant Bangladeshi and Indian food experience with superb delivery service. The concept of Bangkok Dinner Restaurant arose from a need to challenge the current norm in local takeaway delivery service with several years of business experience.

Bangkok Dinner Restaurant aim to give you food cooked with highest standards, assuring the highest level of hygiene and customer satisfaction and committed to delivery your food in warm and within 20 minutes of cooking into your door steps.

Please Note: We do not use any artificial colouring; we do not use any almonds on any items unless requested by any of our customers.

We offer free delivery within 3 mile radius. Any order for delivery outside of the free delivery zone will cost extra delivery charge.
