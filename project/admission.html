Admission Eligibility

Candidates seeking admission must apply in prescribed application Form which can be collected on payment of Tk. 1000.00 Candidates having 2nd division or at least GPA 2.50 in both SSC & HSC examinations, or their equivalents are eligible to apply for admission into Honours program in Law,English, Sociology and Business Administration. For B.Sc in (Computer Science & Engineering), B.Sc.( Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering) and B.Sc (Civil Engineering) programs, candidate must have physics & Mathematics at their HSC level.For Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm.) program, candidates must have Biology & Chemistry at their HSC level.For LL.B(2 years),M.A English (2.5 Years), MSS Sociology (2 Years), MHRL, and MBA programs, the applicants must have at least Bachelor Degree with GPA-2.00 (minimum).

Required Documents

For Undergraduate/ Honor’s Program:
* Original Transcript & Certificate of S.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies.
* Original Transcript & Certificate of H.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies.
* 5 Copies of Pass Port size Photograph.

For Master’s / Graduate Program:
* Original Transcript & Certificate of S.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies.
* Original Transcript & Certificate of H.S.C. or Equivalent with 2 photocopies.
* Original Transcript & Certificate of Undergraduate/ Honor’s Program with 2 photocopies.
* 5 Copies of Pass Port size Photograph.

Financial Assistance

Result : GPA 5.00 ( Both in SSC & HSC) : 50 % of Tuition Fees.
GPA 4.50 ( Both in SSC & HSC) : 25 % of Tuition Fees.
GPA 4.00 ( Both in SSC & HSC) : 10 % of Tuition Fees.

Female Student : 15 % of Tuition Fees.

N.B. : Only one type of waiver will be given .Applicable only for 4 years Honours Courses ( Except CSE, Civil & EETE Evening Shift).
