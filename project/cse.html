Welcome to Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Welcome to Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Dhaka International University. Department of Computer Science and Engineering is one of the leading departments of Dhaka International University. It started the journey in 1996. Two shifts are simultaneously run here as B.Sc. program: Day shift (For H.S.C students) and Evening Shift (For Diploma students). At the current stage, 40th batch is running as Day shift and 55th batch is running as Evening shift. Department also provides M.Sc. degree (1.5 year) as Thesis and Project based. The former students of CSE department have formed DIU CSE Alumni Association.
