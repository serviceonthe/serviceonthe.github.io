Welcome to Dhaka International University Alumni

Dhaka International University (DIU) is recognized in independent government assessments as one of top graded universities in Bangladesh. The university has been founded by Dhaka International University Group with the approval of the Ministry of Education under the Private University Act of 1992 and its amendment in 1998 and Dhaka International University came into being on 7th April 1995, the University today combines impressive modern facilities and a dynamic approach to teaching and research with its proud heritage of service and achievement.
