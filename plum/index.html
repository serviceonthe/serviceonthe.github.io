FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How quickly can I sell my property?

Our aim is to help you sell your house as quickly as possible. The time-scales will vary depending on your circumstances, the more information you provide us with initially the quicker the process will be.

How quickly will I be paid?

You will be paid immediately into your designated bank account upon completion of the sale.

Are there any up front fees to sell my home with you?

No, there are no fees when selling your home through Hummingbird Property Buyers. You won’t have to pay for the valuation and we will cover your legal fee’s.

Is the process completely confidential?

Yes, the process is entirely confidential and we will only share information that you authorise us to share. We will need to instruct a surveyor and solicitor, but they are also committed to maintaining confidentiality.

Will I need a solicitor?

Yes, you will need a solicitor to process the sale of your property. We can help by recommending one free of charge to ensure a smooth, quick service. You are welcome to use your own solicitor, however this means you will be responsible for their fee’s and we will have less control over the time it takes to process the paperwork.

What if my house is in poor condition?

We aim to buy any house regardless of condition. Our offer will reflect the value of the house in its current condition and the speed you are looking to sell. We pride ourselves on being able to establish the value of any property.

Can you help if I am facing repossession or are in arrears?

Yes, we will work with you to buy your house as quickly as possible, in order to avoid repossession. We can negotiate with your mortgage provider and/or other creditors to delay repossession. If repossession proceedings have been issued it is important that you act fast so we can provide you with the best possible help. Contact us immediately.

Why should I sell through Hummingbird Property Buyers?

We are a customer focused service with local property knowledge and expertise. We will be able to buy your property at a realistic price, in the quickest time possible whilst avoiding the traditional costs and hassle of selling on the open market. We aim to make the entire process as simple and convenient as possible for you.
