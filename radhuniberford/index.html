Welcome To Radhuni Cafe Bar & Grill At russell park, The Embankment, Bedford


Fully refurbished and open, Radhuni Cafe Bar & Grill Restaurant the Embankment Bedford, serves high quality, freshly prepared cuisine lunch and evening. Located on the parkside at Russell Park, Bedford, Radhuni is ideally located for those using the park facilities as well as local residents of the Embankment and general Bedford area. Our menu features traditional Indian fine dining served in the restaurant or enjoy on the terrace weather permitting.

We cater for all your dining requirements from a light snack to early evening meal. Our day menu features traditional light snacks and early after meals, or alternatively simply relax in the dedicated coffee bar area.

Join us for the evening and relax in our dedicated bar area before selecting from our full evening menu featuring speciality dishes to cater for everyones pallet. Catering for large parties and intimate evenings out in one of our secluded booths, Radhuni is ideal for everyone whatever the occasion - and once again weather permitting the terrace makes a beautiful setting for any summers evening entertainment. We have full parking facilities and are easily located on the Embankment waterside area of Bedford.

Come and join us, see the new facilities and sample from our extensive menu. Perhaps visit one of our regular entertainment evenings featuring live entertainment and three course meal... follow our events section for further information and to subscribe to our announcements.
