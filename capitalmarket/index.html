Welcome to Bangladesh Capital Market Fair 2013

The Bangladesh Capital Market Fair 2013 is going to be the leading capital marketing fair aimed at the UK based fund managers, private investors and Non-Resident Bangladeshis (NRBs). This is the third such fair going to be a held in London (UK). Over the last two years this fair has become, the main platform for the Stock Exchange Members from Bangladesh to promote their companies and services and encourage people to invest in the capital market.

After the successful hosting of this fair in London last year in association with the Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE), the Curry Life Events, once again organising this fair.

The organiser of this fair will invite a select group of Stock Brokers with clear track record and credibility to showcase their products and services to Bangladeshis in the UK. At the same time, the organiser intends to generate absolute confidence in the minds of the investors willing to invest in the capital market in Bangladesh.

The programme will includes some exclusive meetings with fund managers, private equity companies and high net worth NRBs.

Bangladeshis form one of the UK's largest immigrant groups, and are also one of its youngest and fastest-growing communities. The population of Bangladeshis in Britain has grown steadily over the years. Current estimates suggest that there are approximately 600,000 Bangladeshis residing in the UK.

The Bangladeshi community is a thriving community intent on establishing themselves in trade and commerce in both the UK and Bangladesh.

The BCMF 2013 will be held in the heart of the British Bangladeshi community in the East End of London. We will therefore immediately have access to thousands of British Bangladeshi potential clients.
