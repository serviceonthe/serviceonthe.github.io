welcome to

Millbank Spice in SW1

Welcome to Millbank Spice, the finest Indian cuisine located in Vauxhall Bridge Road, London Sw1. We warmly welcome you to try an elegant Bangladeshi and Indian dining experience with superb service. Enjoy exquisite traditional Indian food as its finest in this contemporary style restaurant. The concept of Millbank spice arose from a need to challenge the current norm in local dining scene with several years of business experience.

We the management have highly talented and experiecned chefs that aim to provide a tantalising array of tastes and aromas from the heart of the Indian Subcontinet.

We are open seven days a week from 12pm - 2.30pm and 6PM to Midnight. 6pm to 11:30am (Friday & Saturday). We also caters for events. If you are planning a special event such a birthday party or an office party then why not place your order with Millbank Spice, The true home of Indian food in SW1.
