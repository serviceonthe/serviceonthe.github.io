Welcome to Nadir Indian Cuisine

Welcome to Nadir Indian Cuisine, the finest Indian restaurant located in Sittingbourne ME10 2AR. We warmly welcome you to try an elegant Bangladeshi and Indian dining experience with superb service. Enjoy exquisite traditional Indian food as its finest in this contemporary style restaurant.

The concept of Nadir Cuisine arose from a need to challenge the current norm in local dining scene with several years of business experience.

We the management have highly talented and experienced chefs that aim to provide a tantalizing array of tastes and aromas from the heart of the Indian Subcontinent.

We are open seven days a week including Bank holidays and caters for events. If you are planning a special event such a birthday party or an office party then why not place your order with Nadir Indian Restaurant. The true home of Indian food in Sittingbourne.
