SHAMSUL ISLAM

Entrepreneur

Shamsul Islam is a British Bangladeshi entrepreneur with a long experience in organising media events, exhibitions and road shows. He is the Director, Training and Development, for the British Fusion Food Festival.

Having completed his MBA at Oxford Brookes University, Mr. Islam serves as a director of the Nandan Group of Companies, which owns Nandan Megashop chain stores as well as the Nandan Park. He is also a Director for SAFE Solution offering training and consultancy in the areas of food and health and safety.
