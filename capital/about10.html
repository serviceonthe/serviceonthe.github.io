MUHIBUR RAHMAN MUHIB

Advisor

Muhibur Rahman Muhib is a leading British-Bangladeshi businessman. He came to the United Kingdom in 1972. He first started his business career in design and textile industry. Then on a later stage he decided that the catering industry was his best bet to make his fortune and opened his first restaurant in the East End of London.

After several successful years in theindustry Mr Muhib established a portfolio of restaurants in and around London.As well as restaurants Mr Muhib has other business interests in both the UK andBangladesh ranging from property development, real estate, manufacturing.

Mr Muhib is a much-respected member of the Bangladeshi community in the UK and is involved in numerous charity organisations. He is a trustee of the Bangladesh Centre in London. He is also the founder Executive Director of UK Bangladesh Business Council (UKBBC).
